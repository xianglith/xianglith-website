About Us

XIANG LI EMPLOYMENT (THAILAND) CO., LTD. is a labor
contracting service company dedicated to providing high-quality
workforce solutions for business operators. We emphasize efficiency in recruitment
and uphold service standards with customer satisfaction as our priority.
Our policies include:
Delivering services that meet customer needs by recruiting suitable personnel quickly and with high quality, tailored to every workplace.
Supporting and assisting recruited workers, such as offering consultation on work-related issues and helping them adapt to their workplace.
Providing services with honesty and integrity.
Ensuring fair treatment and employment practices for all employees.
